RUKAImenue 

     
  

  

    They distributed in Mau-lin Shiang in Kaohsiung 
County, Wu-tai Country in Pin-don Shiang, and 
Don-hsing Shiang in Tai-don County. The population 
is about 12,000. 

Mainly the noble and common people hierarchy maintains 
the social organization. The nobles enjoy the 
privileges of the blood superiority and the ownership 
of lands. 

As for the common people, they promote their 
social status by developing their leading ability, 
by efforts in agriculture, and by marriage. 
The eldest son inherits Rukai family.


Harvest Festival: Bakig millet biscuits is an important part of the festival. The Rukai people predict the weather and the amount of rainfall based on the relative moistness or dryness of the baked biscuits. It is held in August every year to welcome the beginning of the next year's agricultural cycle.
