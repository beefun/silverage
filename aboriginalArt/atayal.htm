ATAYALmenue 

    
  

  

    
	 The traditional distribution of Atayal locates on the mid-northern mountain 
      areas of Taiwan, includes the northern part of Pu-li-Hua-lian. The population 
      at present is approximate thousand or so, which is the biggest apart from 
      Amis. The traditional life style is hunting and farming. The material culture 
      is distinguished for its weaving techniques that is quite complicated. The 
      color of the cloth is delicate. Red color symbolizes blood, which is vital 
      and can keep away the evil. Tattoo on their faces is one of the Atayal customs. 
      Mainly the ancestral workship group maintains the social organization. The 
      specialty of singing and dancing activity is the gan and it's dancing.



	Ancestor Festival: After the millet harvest in the summer,
	 the Atayal people gather to dance and sing with bamboo sticks
	 in their hands in order to worship their ancestors and to pray
	 for a good weather and a bountiful millet harvest.
