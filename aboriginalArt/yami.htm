YAMImenue 

    
  

  

    They located in the Lan-yu island of Tai-don. 
It is the only oceanic ethnic group in Taiwan.

Their traditional costume is the simplest 
among the aboriginals, so they remained the 
traditional culture well. In the Yami culture, 
there is no leadership system. If there is a 
dispute between people, the relatives are called 
on to solve it. The fathers or men's groups as 
well as the fishing community organize the 
community affairs. 



The material in the culture is shown to the 
abundant. The techniques of building and carving 
the boats, the making of silvery utensils, 
the pottery, and the muddolls are the most characteristic. 

They have basements in their houses. They are warm 
in the winter and cool in the summer even when 
there is strong wind. The rituals are held according to 
the activities of capturing the flying fish, they are 
also characterized for the belief of regarding the 
flying fish as their sacred symbol and they regard the 
flying fish as holy. 



The Yami women use their hair a great deal when they dance.
It is very attractive and charming. The dancing of the men 
presents both power and strength, which is very impressive.



Boat Ceremony(flying fish festival): The Yami people traditionally made their living from the sea, and a fishing boat is considered a man's most important possession. This ceremony celebrates the first launching of a newly carved boat. These are always important events for all of Orchid Island.
