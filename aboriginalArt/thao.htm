    Thao is distributed from Yuchr Shang to Shuelli Shang 
in Nantou County. The population is about three thousand. 
Deu to the Executive Yuan, they had mergered to Tsou. 
In recent years, the racial conscious were strongly responded 
and the require from them. Finally, they are formal named 
the tenth ethic group of the Taiwan's aboriginal in year ninty, 
September twenty second.



There is a legend that the Thao 
found Sun Moon Lake while they were hunting a white deer. 
Therefore, they moved to there. The specialty of their culture is the social organization 
in their village is by the patrilateral clan. However, 
they were deeply influenced by Han's culture; they still 
remained their own parts. Each family's house hangs, their 
ancestors' bag on their left corner inside their building 
and no other ethic groups have this specialty. 

Thao's chief is the decider and the judger of the social 
business in normal time. The position is usually inheritance 
to the eldest son.
